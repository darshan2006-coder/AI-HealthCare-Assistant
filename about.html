What is HealthBot?

HealthBot is an advanced AI-powered healthcare assistant designed to provide personalized health information and symptom analysis. Our intelligent chatbot uses machine learning to understand your health concerns and provide relevant, helpful responses based on medical knowledge.

Supported Symptoms & Conditions

HealthBot is trained to provide information about a wide range of symptoms and conditions, including:

Common Symptoms

  • Fever and high temperature
  • Headache and migraines
  • Cough and respiratory issues
  • Fatigue and weakness
  • Nausea and vomiting

Skin & Allergies

  • Itching and skin irritation
  • Skin rash and inflammation
  • Continuous sneezing
  • Eye irritation
  • Runny nose and congestion

Pain & Discomfort

  • Chest pain and discomfort
  • Abdominal and stomach pain
  • Knee pain and joint issues
  • Muscle weakness
  • Loss of balance

Mental Health

  • Depression and mood changes
  • Irritability and stress
  • Sleep disturbances
  • Anxiety symptoms
  • Cognitive concerns
